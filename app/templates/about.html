About Customer Segmentation

This application uses Machine Learning (KMeans Clustering) to segment customers into distinct groups based on their annual income and spending behavior. By understanding these segments, businesses can:

  • Target marketing campaigns more effectively
  • Develop personalized product recommendations
  • Optimize pricing strategies for different customer groups
  • Improve customer retention and satisfaction
  • Maximize revenue through better customer understanding

Technical Details

  • Algorithm: KMeans Clustering
  • Features: Annual Income, Spending Score
  • Preprocessing: StandardScaler normalization
  • Initialization: k-means++
  • Number of Clusters: 5 (optimized using Elbow Method & Silhouette Score)
